PubMed²
PubMed is a popular website to query the research litterature related to medicine and life science. PubMed² is an experiment exploring new interactive ways to vizualize the search results from PubMed on tablets, smart phones and classical computers.
How to use: Enter a normal PubMed or Google query, based on keywords. You can add the tag #review if you want to focus your search only on review articles.
